Meet the team

Dare Tunji Arigbede 
Chief Executive Officer

Dare Tunji Arigbede is the managing director and Chief Executive officer of Castle Mews Limited, a company poised with the vision to add values to the real estate industry by building choice and affordable homes. Dare hails from Ekiti state of Nigeria. A graduate of Economics, from the Lagos State University of Nigeria. Dare has attended several management and leadership training.

Sikeoye Arigbede
Heads of sales and marketing 

Sikeoye Arigbede or Sike as she is fondly called is a graduate of finance from the prestigious Federal University of Ilorin, Ilorin Kwara state. A certified project manager, with over a decade experience in the construction and real estate sector. Sikeoye has had frontline service experience with Lenox and Blair Limited where she was in charge of the development of the Lenox Mall at the heart of Lekki Phase 1.
